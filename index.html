This is an old version of the sbgnbricks website, and we continue maintaining it mainly for educational purposes: the Process Description, Activity Flow and Entity Relationship pages offer examples with explanations, and the SBGN Bricks Dictionary page allows comparing the expressions of biological concepts in the tree SBGN languages.
